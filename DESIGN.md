# Design DNA — Croisonido

## Visual Personality: warm-luxurious
## Layout: storytelling
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary (Espresso): #1A0F0A → `oklch(0.13 0.03 40)`
- Accent (Amber Gold): #E8A045 → `oklch(0.72 0.14 60)`
- Surface Light (Cream Parchment): #F5EBD8 → `oklch(0.93 0.03 70)`
- Text Main (Warm White): `oklch(0.90 0.02 70)`

**Surface scale (dark theme — flipped):**
- surface-50: deep espresso near-black `oklch(0.10 0.025 35)`
- surface-100: dark chocolate `oklch(0.14 0.025 38)`
- surface-200: rich brown `oklch(0.20 0.025 40)`
- surface-800: warm parchment `oklch(0.88 0.03 70)`
- surface-900: cream `oklch(0.93 0.03 68)`
- surface-950: near-white cream `oklch(0.96 0.02 65)`

### Font Pairing
- Display: Cormorant Garamond — weights 300, 500, 700 (italic) — for dramatic headline elegance
- Body: Montserrat — weights 300, 400, 500 — for clean modern readability

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Waveform divider motif** — a subtle SVG sine wave divider between sections, evoking the DJ set / audio groove language — rendered in amber at ~15% opacity
2. **Immersive dark hero** — near-black espresso base with layered amber/gold radial glows offset to upper-right (evokes golden hour light), grain texture overlay for a "warm film" feel — NO white or light backgrounds anywhere above the fold
3. **Dramatic typographic scale contrast** — Cormorant Garamond at 96–120px on hero (italic, thin weight) against tight 13px Montserrat uppercase tracking labels — the tension between old-world type and precision lettering creates the "exclusive but inviting" brand feel
4. **Amber gradient underline accents** — section headings receive a short amber gradient bar below (not a full underline, a decorative em-dash width marker) to reinforce the golden pastry color story
